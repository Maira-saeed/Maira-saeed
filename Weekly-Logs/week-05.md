##  What I Learned

In this week, I learned about the Code of Conduct in computing, which defines ethical principles and professional standards for IT professionals.

I understood that computing professionals must follow ethical guidelines because their work can affect society, users, and organizations.

I studied three main professional bodies:

### IEEE Code of Ethics
IEEE focuses on engineering ethics and emphasizes:
- Public safety and welfare  
- Honesty in professional work  
- Professional responsibility  
- Avoiding conflicts of interest  

### ACM Code of Ethics
ACM focuses on computing ethics and highlights:
- Avoiding harm to users  
- Protecting privacy and data  
- Being honest and fair  
- Respecting users  

### BCS Code of Conduct
BCS focuses on IT professionals and includes:
- Public interest and safety  
- Professional integrity  
- Duty to employer  
- Confidentiality  

I also learned that all three codes share common values such as honesty, fairness, responsibility, and public safety.

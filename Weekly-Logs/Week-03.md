- Professional Conduct and Ethics  
- Diversity in Technology

---

##  What I Learned

In this week, I learned that workplace culture plays a significant role in shaping how individuals behave, collaborate, and contribute in professional environments. In the technology sector, a positive culture promotes innovation, teamwork, and ethical practices.

I understood that professional conduct refers to behaving responsibly and ethically according to organizational and professional standards. A technology professional must demonstrate integrity, responsibility, competence, and respect for others. Protecting user privacy, ensuring software quality, and being honest in work are essential responsibilities.

Another important concept was diversity in technology. Diversity means having people from different backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives. It enhances creativity, improves problem-solving, and leads to better product design.

I also learned about inclusion, which ensures that all individuals feel valued, respected, and able to contribute fully. Inclusion promotes equal opportunities, psychological safety, and accessibility in the workplace.

Overall, this lecture highlighted that professional conduct, diversity, and inclusion are interconnected and essential for a healthy and productive work environment.
